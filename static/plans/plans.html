Open Days
WHAT'S ON OFFER?
The Open Days give you an insight into what your course will be like, the facilities you will have access to and the staff who will be teaching you, plus you'll have the chance to explore the University campus.
In the morning, you will have the chance to find out more about the campus. You will be able to go on city coach tours, walking tours, halls tours, and attend housing and finance talks. You can visit our Students' Union, Library and Sport and Recreation Department.
In the afternoon, you will visit your department and find out about your course in more detail. If you're unsure of where to go for this, please ask at the registration desk.

CITY AND CAMPUS TOUR
Our Student Marketing Assistants will be on hand to take you on tours of the city, campus and halls.
  • City coach tours: depart from the Union Building at 9.15am, then every hour with the last tour at 12.15pm. Tours last 50 minutes.
  • Walking tours: depart from the Nuffield Advice Centre at 9am, then every 15 minutes. The last tour leaves at 12.15pm. Tours last 45 minutes.
  • Halls of residence tours: depart from the main entrance of the halls. Tours last 20 minutes starting at 9am, with the last tour leaving at 12.45pm. There will also be an opportunity to visit a hall of residence between 3pm and 5pm. Please ask at the registration desk which halls are open.
  • Langstone Student Village Halls tours: An opportunity to view our Langstone halls of residence will be available (Saturday events only). Coaches will be departing at 10am and 11am from the Union Building and tours will last 50 minutes. Between 3pm and 5pm, visitors can make their own way to Langstone Student Village, PO4 8LW, to view accommodation. Parking is available on site, charges will apply.
SUPPORT SERVICES TALKS
These are ideal for finding out about financial support and the accommodation application process.
Venue: Main Hall, Nuffield Advice Centre
Accommodation talk: 10am and 11.15am, duration 30 minutes
Finance talk: 10.30am and 11.45am, duration 45 minutes
Both will include a Q&A session.
You can also visit the teams on a drop-in basis throughout the day in their offices in the Nuffield Advice Centre (only on Wednesday events).
